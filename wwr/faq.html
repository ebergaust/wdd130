Routes

The primary and only rapid route is the one that follows the river Not A Thing. The rapids increase in intensity the further down the lake which allows a high veriety of difficulty on the single route.

Rapids

Rapids are classified from One to Six. One being calm waters with ripples and Six being waterfalls. On the part of Not A Thing that we opperate there is: One Class 5, Three Class 4, Seven Class 3, and an unknown amount of Class 1 and 2.
